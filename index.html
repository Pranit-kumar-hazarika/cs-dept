ABOUT US

The Department of Computer Science, Bahona College (est. 2023), is dedicated to nurturing tech-savvy minds through a 4-year B.Sc. program under Dibrugarh University. With modern ICT-enabled classrooms, air-conditioned labs, and a focus on programming, OS, and hardware, we empower students for high-demand careers.

We promote hands-on learning through internships, seminars, field trips, and more—guided by friendly faculty in a dynamic, future-ready environment.
