About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science at Bahona College, established in 2023, represents a new era of technological education in Assam. We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of tech innovators through our comprehensive 4-year B.Sc. program under Dibrugarh University.

Our department emphasizes practical learning through industry internships, research seminars, educational field trips, and hands-on projects. Students benefit from personalized mentorship by our expert faculty in a dynamic, future-ready learning environment that prepares them for the evolving technology landscape.

Why Choose Our Department?

Experience cutting-edge education with ICT-enabled smart classrooms, advanced computer laboratories, and personalized mentorship from industry-experienced faculty members who are committed to your success.

Benefits of Joining Us

Unlock opportunities through industry internships, research collaborations, coding competitions, hackathons, and comprehensive skill-building programs designed to make you industry-ready.

Welcome to the Computer Science Department of Bahona College. Empowering students through knowledge, innovation, and technology.
