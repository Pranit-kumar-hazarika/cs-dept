ABOUT US

The Department of Computer Science, Bahona College (est. 2023), is dedicated to nurturing tech-savvy minds through a 4-year B.Sc. program under Dibrugarh University.

We promote hands-on learning through internships, seminars, field trips, and more—guided by friendly faculty in a dynamic, future-ready environment.

Why Choose Our Department?

We provide ICT-enabled classrooms, advanced labs, and mentorship from expert faculty.

Benefits of Joining Us

Opportunities in internships, research, hackathons, and skill-building programs.
